I made this app as a feature suggestion for allrecipes.com. It's intended as a cost/benefit comparison of going to a restaurant for a meal, versus cooking it. With access to more comprehensive API information, you could get an exact price analysis of the cost of labor time and ingredients, as well as the exact menu item at the restaurant. Some dishes you're just not going to prepare as well as the experts, and you would spend an excess of time and money on a difficult preparation or expensive ingredients. But sometimes you might be denying yourself a fulfilling experience. Cooking is an important communal activity that is often overlooked in favor of a cheap, quick meal!
